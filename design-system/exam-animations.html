Focused-exam animations — starter set

Five seed renderers for the exam registry. The design spec defines ~20 keyed renderers plus a build-time custom-generation path for findings outside the library — this is the database's first cut, not its limit.
Pupils
pupil-reaction · {leftMm, leftReact, rightMm, rightReact}
Swinging penlight: reactive pupil constricts under the light, fixed pupil never reacts.
Breathing
breathing · {rate, pattern, retractions}
Side-on supine abdomen at the monitor RR; retractions appear only when present; tone tracks SpO₂.
Capillary refill
cap-refill · {seconds, site}
Examiner pinches the fingertip; nailbed blanches, then refills over the finding's seconds.
Skin inspection
skin-inspect · {findings:[{type, location, severity}]}
Pans across clear skin and stops on the lesion (bruise / laceration / rash / petechiae).
